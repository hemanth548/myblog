Web Design

Web design is the process of creating websites. It encompasses several different aspects, including goal identification, Sitemap and wireframe creation, webpage layout, prototying, content production, and testing.

Graphic Design

Graphic design is the process of visual communication and problem-solving through the use of typography, iconography and illustration. This is a subset of visual communication and communication design

Mobile App Design

Mobile app design is the task of designing mobile applications. What unifies them is the need of optimal usability, accessibility and overall user experience. In short, addressing the above concerns.

Web Development

Web development refers to building, creating, and maintaining the content of websites by using a variety of coding languages. A web developer may also be involved in writing web scripting using web scripting languages.
