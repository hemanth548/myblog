Voyaah - Customized Trip Planner

As the perception of experiential travel and authentic luxury gathered steam with years and years of being on the road, Voyaah realized the gap. New vistas opened up like receiving postcards from unknown lands and we journeyed on...

Challange

Most travel sites lead with transactional options – Bookings and Packages. The Challange is to lead with Content, with transactional options as supplemental to the content experience, provide a robust and scaleable engagement to allow for curated place.

Our Goals

The company provides online travel services including flight tickets, domestic and international holiday packages, hotel reservations, activity bookings and luxury staycations. With decent downloads on the Android PlayStore, Apple store and an good rating stars, the service is widely used as an application and the website is easy to navigate too and contains every relevant feature that the user would require in the travel niche.
  1. Engage with the target customers.
  2. Make it fast and easy to use for everyone, everywhere.
  3. Give customers more control over their time and money.
  4. Create a platform for innovation and deeper engagement

User & Strategy

The campaign is called Voyage, is the basic ideology behind of marketing strategy to target all those half-made and never-successful plans that college-going kids and working freshers make. This campaign is here with a group that offers that even the introverts of the group cannot refuse and make memories out of the “Goa Trip that never happened”. Though there are online platforms already offers a lot of daily coupons, the communication of the same is very family-oriented and this campaign makes it more youthful and dynamic. The digital marketing campaign focuses on setting up VR Booths where three people can enter at once and experience a “friend-cation” at one of the 5 spots that come at the top of a college student’s travel list- Goa, Manali and Leh. The student will experience the temperatures, sonic range, and activities virtually. They can click pictures, make boomerangs or videos within the tunnel and post them up with a cool hashtag for a feature on our page. This will ensure that we deviates from the business-trips or family vacations and inculcate a trendy friend-cation vibe in its communication.

Solution

The solution is a combination of Technology and UX elements combining to provide a robust and scaleable engagement: On the technology side, we propose to create a middleware platform in order to present the APIs and allow for curation to take place.
 Udai Gali, UX Lead

This brand helps its cutomers in creating a platform for travel enthusiasts and well-planned trips and gives great control over their time and money
