What I Do

User Experience (UX) research and design is multi-disciplinary field, the objective of which is to shape the entire experience of using a product. User experience is a combination of usability, usefulness, visual communication and design.

User Experience Design

UX Design is User-Centered, is the process used to determine what the experience will be like when a user interacts with your product.

  • Plan, implement new user interface designs.
  • Combine creativity with an awareness of the design elements.

User Experience Research

UX Researcher will be responsible for evaluating the usability, conceptualizing and forming product and website improvements and features.

Uncovering user behaviors, needs, and motivations in order to design products and services that provide value is the crux of user experience research. We will collaborate across development, design, and marketing teams to evaluate current and upcoming user research needs that help to improve product definition.
